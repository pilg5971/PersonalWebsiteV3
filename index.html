About Me

"For me, coding became a way to live my passions and help people develop their dream projects."

Throughout my career, I always found myself drifting away from the more boilerplate, static systematology within coding. I ventured into the realm of front-end development near the latter half of college, but truly my story is that of continuous education and passion.

Whether it's wireframing, UI/UX design, front-end development, or creating graphic designs through Inkscape - I've been working within the creative industry of CS for years. Responsive designs are crucial within my design philosophy, alongside my viewpoint in taking extra time to produce nothing but my best work.
